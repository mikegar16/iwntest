WHO IS INFINITE WIRELESS & NETWORKING

We are a local wireless internet provider established in Spanish Lookout with experience of over a decade. Our goal is to provide fast and reliable High Speed Internet to customers even in the most remote areas. Last year we decided to continue expanding to the Cayo District and surrounding villages where as a result constructed over 30 towers. We're always try to be involved in the community therefore we provided some police stations and schools with free internet connectivity to show our appreciation to the police and the importance of Technology in Education.

We Provide Service to the Following Areas:

Spanish Lookout Camalote Roaring Creek
Duck Run 1,2 & 3 Blackman Eddy Unitedville
Buena Vista Riverwalk San Antonio
Santa Familia Santa Elena San Ignacio

INSTALLATION PROCESS & REQUIREMENTS

Here at Infinite Wireless & Networking our customers' request are all treated as high priority, from the first contact with Customer we prepare equipment, then perform an onsite survey to check for quality of signal and then proceed to install equipment at customers premises if survey is successful. Before leaving customer premises our technicians, test and approve internet connectivity at speed requested by the customer.
